Best Practices

Tips for Better Results

Follow these tips for better product identification and price comparison.

Good Images
  • Use clear, well-lit photos
  • Make sure the brand name is visible
  • Keep the product label facing the camera
  • Use high-resolution images
  • Use plain or simple backgrounds
  • Keep the product as the main object in the image
Avoid These Issues
  • Blurry or out-of-focus photos
  • Dark or poorly lit images
  • Rotated or tilted labels
  • Heavily curved packaging
  • Background clutter
  • Very small or distant product shots
